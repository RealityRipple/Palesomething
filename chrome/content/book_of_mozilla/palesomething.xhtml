There is a place of which I'm told, where rivers sun with shimmering gold. The grandest sights, lo and behold; the vineyards grow to heights untold.
And those who live to tell their tales of peering through the Dragon's scales will live in search to no avail until the wind tears down their sails.

Any map drawn by mortal men couldn't help you comprehend. But trinkets carved in alabaster will show you how to get there faster.
When the last light of the setting sun casts their shadows where there are none, follow the trail with broken hands, and only then will you understand:

Once you leave you can never come back again

S. Ambarian, 2008
